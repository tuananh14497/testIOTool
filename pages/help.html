How to get your cookie:

Step 1:

Open TestIO Login Page

(Browser incognito mode is recommand)

Steps 2:

Right Click anywhere on the page and Select "Inspect"

Step 3:

Select "Network" Tab on the "DevTools" window

Step 4:

Back to Login Page and Login as normal

Step 5:

Look for "Login" on the left Side bar of the "DevTools" window and click it

Step 6:

Under "Response Cookie", double click and copy the "Value"
